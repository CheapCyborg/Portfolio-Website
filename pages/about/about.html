I have been in love with technology as long as I can remember and have always wanted to work with it in some way. I discovered programming in middle school and began making small Flash games with ActionScript for me and my friends to play. Eventually, I stumbled upon HTML and began making basic websites and embedded my YouTube videos on it. I have always loved diving into new things that interest me head first and just learning as I go along, I have picked up countless skills over the years; The main one being to learn new things very quickly. I have now graduated from a Full Stack Development boot camp and while I may lack some of the required experience, I am extremely eager to learn and get my foot in the door! I truly think technology has the power to make everyone's lives so much better and I want more than anything to eventually give to that and if given the opportunity, will definitely not put it to waste.
